Drawing a face with shadows

›

Level: Experienced

By Sara Herlet

This tutorial has three videos to it. Here you can truly see the power of drawing digitally. The way the face can get altered with the selections tool. You also see the power of the easy erasing, which let you change the pose quickly and easily without trace of the previous lines.

Try this:

  • Draw the facial features and play with the face shape
  • Add hair and start on drawing the body
  • Add other elements with l leaves and branches
  • Add a new layer where you can add color and color it in
  • Add shadow but beware where you put it you have to keep the leaves in mind

TIP: Play with backgrounds to give the drawing a different vibe.

Try to follow the video and pause when necessary, you can try to draw the same girl or find your own style.

Background and shadow

A great way to add life to your drawing is to play with the background and shadows. When placing shadow always keep in mind where the light is hitting and what element might block for the light.
