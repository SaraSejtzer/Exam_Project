Drawing eyes

›

Level: Never drawn

By S. Sejtzer

Here is a quick video of four sketches of eyes all with different expressions. They are drawn with the program Procreate, in a single layer. It's always a good idea to find a reference picture to look at while drawing especially when you are new in the field.

Try this:

  • Start by drawing the outline of the eyes, play around until you find a shape you like
  • Draw the inner circle to the eyes
  • Draw the pupils and add some highlight to add dimension
  • Add an upper lid or eyeliner to finish the eyes up

TIP: Try to draw your own eyes with a picture or by looking in the mirror.

Try to follow the video and pause when necessary, you can try to draw the same eyes or find your own style.

Proportions

There are many different ways to go about drawing eyes or a face. You can build everything around the eyes, but you can also draw the head first, try both and find out what you preferer. Here is some drawing of proportions to keep in mind when you draw.
